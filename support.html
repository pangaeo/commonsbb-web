Commons Baseball Companion App

The Commons Baseball app for iOS and Android is a companion tool designed to enhance your tabletop experience. It allows you to:

  • Manage Rosters: Build and organize your teams with ease.
  • Convert Statistics: Automatically convert player statistics into dice roll probabilities.
  • Print Resources: Generate and print probability sheets or labels for your cards.
